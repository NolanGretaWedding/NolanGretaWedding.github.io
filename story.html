A bit about us

Our story

Here is a short version of the story so far. We are grateful for every twist and turn that brought us to this moment.

We met

2014 — Harmony Middle School

We first met in 2014 at Harmony Middle School in sixth grade. We had several classes together including band, where we both played the clarinet.

First Date

August 2018

On our first date, we went tubing down the Potomac River. Nolan managed to get stuck in a tree in the middle of the river. It ended up being a fun and memorable start to our relationship.

The proposal

May 27, 2025 — Seneca Rocks, WV

For our fifth year anniversary in 2023, we took a trip to Seneca Rocks, WV where we had an incredible weekend. Because of that trip, Nolan decided it would be the perfect place to propose. To celebrate our recent college graduations, our friend group took a weekend trip to camp near Seneca Rocks. On a rainy morning hike, Nolan proposed to Greta at the overlook.
