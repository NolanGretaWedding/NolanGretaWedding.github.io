A bit about us

Our story

Here is a short version of the story so far. We are grateful for the many years we've had together and look forward to many more.

We met

2014 — Harmony Middle School

We first met in 2014 at Harmony Middle School. In 6th grade, we had three classes together (that we can remember): band, english, and history. In the summer of 2018, before the start of our sophomore year, we started hanging out more due to a shared friend group. Right before the start of the school year we began dating with a little sequence that kind of goes like this: "Will you go out with me?" "Sure" "Are you sure?" "Eh, why not?" Ladies and gentlemen, that's how it's done.

First Date

August 2018

On our first date, we went tubing down the Potomac River. As all young couples are, we were fairly awkward. Things started to shift for the better when Nolan somehow got stuck in a tree in the middle of the river. Although it was slightly embarrassing, it helped break the ice and bit. Now, it is one of our favorite memories from our early days together.

The proposal

May 27, 2025 — Seneca Rocks, WV

In 2023, we took a trip to Seneca Rocks, West Virginia for away in a small middle-of-nowhere cabin. On this trip, we had an unforgettable time and Seneca Rocks quickly became one of our favorite places. When our friend group planned a graduation camping trip near Seneca Rocks in May 2025, Nolan decided it was the perfect opportunity to propose. With the help of our friends, we managed to keep it a secret (sort of). On the morning of May 27th, we hiked up in the rain and Nolan proposed at the overlook. Although it was rainy, it still made for a beautiful and memorable moment. Thank you to all our friends, especiall Jett our photographer, who helped make it special!
